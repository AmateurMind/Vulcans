Team Vulcans is the premier robotics team and club associated with P.E.S.'s Modern College of Engineering (MCOE) in Shivajinagar, Pune. Founded in 2011, the team focuses on robotics, automation, and competing in prestigious national competitions like ABU Robocon. They are renowned for their technical expertise and achievements in building robots.
